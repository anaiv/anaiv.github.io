Flyer for a credit loans company.

Simple design, with a humanizing effect of the information - by way of a smiling face - to ease the sobriety of a loan company.

Green is the trademark color of the company.

And the backside of the flier has been organized as evenly as possible, so that interested people can digest the information as easily as possible.
