Sirena Dunarii - The Danube Mermaid - is one of the best wines from Vinarte. The design was inspired by the nobility of its quality.

That's why I made a custom mermaid drawing for this particular wine.

Easy to read sans serif fonts that blend with the mermaid drawing.

White background with a golden folio, to highlight the premium quality of the wine.
