The client wanted the logo and the website to express the idea of HD / chameleonic vision

The font is a digital serif that blends in perfectly with the chameleon and its diopters tail

Why a cameleon?

Because a chameleon changes its color and the buying public can enjoy a richer and colorful experience with the client's products.
